How did we get here?

I decided at some point in December to run a recording competition on ilovefuzz.com. For some reason, I mashed up the idea of Iron Chef and got Iron Fuzz. The rules are simple:

  • Record a cover song.
  • Use the "secret ingredient" effect in your song.

My idea that was under constraints, people might be a little more comfortable recording and sharing their creation. (The constraints and light-hearted nature of the competition are there to help, and to prevent the kind of crippling perfectionism that plagues a lot of my own recording efforts.)

ALLEZ FUZZ!

The thread where the competition was discussed follows the format of the Iron Fuzz show -- I've spared you from that style here. (But maybe in the future I will make the website more like a script for the show.)

January turned out pretty great, and I'd like to share everyone's songs with you.
